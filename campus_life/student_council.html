SDC-(Student Development Council)


“SDC” – The Student Council

MASTER MINDS– Future India is a representative structure through which students at TNC involve in the affairs of the college, working in partnership with the management, staff and students acting as a bridge between the college and the students. We aim at providing students an opportunity to acquire communication skills, planning and organisational methodologies which will benefit them in their future. Students making valuable contribution to the betterment of the college and their involvement in the operation of the college is in itself a valuable part of education.

Objectives

  • To provide an opportunity for students to engage in a structured partnership with teachers, students and management in the operation of our college.
  • To improve academic standards and reduce dropout rates
  • To create a sense of ownership of the college and its activities among the student community.
