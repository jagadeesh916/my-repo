Department of Physical Education


About The Department


TNC aims at wholesome development of our students by improving their physical fitness and sporting abilities. The Department of Physical Education ensures the importance of living a healthy lifestyle that remains with them throughout their lives. We have a well-equipped fitness centre which is available forstudents and staff to be utilized from 7 a.m. to 5 p.m. Students are trained in various games in which they participate in the intercollegiate, District, State and National International level tournaments. The College has produced number of State players, National players and University players consistently, every year.

Sports and Games form an integral part of TNC’s curriculum and due importance is given to the field of sports. The Physical Education Department has a well-furnished gym with modern equipment’s spanning an area of 2,400 sq. Ft. and it strives to train the students to be highly talented sportsmen. The college has carefully planned and provided all facilities for athletic events besides Volleyball, Football, Badminton, Basketball, Kabaddi,Table Tennis, Handball, Kho-Kho,Boxing , Archery and Cricket for the students. A Cricket Pitch and a separate net practice pitch were laid during the year 2002-2003. Students are trained and encouraged to participate in many events at many levels so as to bag many prizes and bring glory to the institution which would be an infinite process. Skills like patience, team work, leadership, discipline, learning from failure are developed when students indulge in any type of sports.

Objectives

  • To promote good sportsman with great character through our college sports activities.
  • To inculcate discipline among the students and make them responsible citizens of the country and also to develop the sportive attitude by which students will be able to handle the tough situations of life.
  • To help needy and economically backward students to take part in sports activities for the overall development of the students.
