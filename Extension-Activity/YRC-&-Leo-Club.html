YRC & LEO CLUB


The YRCS club of the college prepares the youth to be trained and encouraged to touch the lives of the needy and the helpless thereby a making a difference in their lives. It exposes them to understand and accept civic responsibilities, and act accordingly with humanitarian concern. It motivates the students to develop friendly relationship without any discrimination. It promotes community service through invaluable training and reaches its ideals through its extended activities.

Objectives

  • To imbibe in youth the Protection of health and life Service to the sick and suffering
  • To substantially increase its contribution to the promotion of human dignity and reduction of death due to disasters and diseases/health emergencies in line with
  • To Promote national and international friendship to develop the mental and moral capacity of the youth.
